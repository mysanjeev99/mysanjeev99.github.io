Wireless Power Transfer using electromagnetic induction

Wireless Power Transfer using electromagnetic induction principle. When there is a continuous flow of electricity in a circular coil and another circular coil placed parallel to the first coil there will be electricity induced into second coil from the first coil, thus electricity is transmitted without any contact.
